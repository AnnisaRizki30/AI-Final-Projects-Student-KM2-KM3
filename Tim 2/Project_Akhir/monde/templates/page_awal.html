What Is MonkeyPox?

Monkeypox is a viral zoonosis (a virus transmitted to humans from animals). It is caused by the monkeypox virus which belongs to the Orthopoxvirus genus of the Poxviridae family. Monkeypox virus is transmitted from one person to another by close contact with lesions, body fluids, respiratory droplets and contaminated materials such as bedding.
